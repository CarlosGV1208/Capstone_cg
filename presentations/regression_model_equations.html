Regression Model Specifications

Model 2: Interactions (No Lags)

$$ \begin{aligned} \log(ECI_{it}) = \beta_0 &+ \beta_1 \log(HCI_{it}) + \beta_2 \log(GFCF_{it}) + \beta_3 \log(Production_{it}) \\ &+ \beta_4 PoliticalStability_{it} + \beta_5 RuleOfLaw_{it} + \beta_6 Trade_{it} \\ &+ \beta_7 [\log(HCI_{it}) \times \log(Production_{it})] \\ &+ \beta_8 [\log(GFCF_{it}) \times \log(Production_{it})] \\ &+ \epsilon_{it} \end{aligned} $$
Note: Interaction terms are mean-centered. Standard errors clustered by country.

Model 3: With Lagged ECI

$$ \begin{aligned} \log(ECI_{it}) = \beta_0 &+ \beta_1 \log(HCI_{it}) + \beta_2 \log(GFCF_{it}) + \beta_3 \log(Production_{it}) \\ &+ \beta_4 PoliticalStability_{it} + \beta_5 RuleOfLaw_{it} + \beta_6 Trade_{it} \\ &+ \beta_7 [\log(HCI_{it}) \times \log(Production_{it})] \\ &+ \beta_8 [\log(GFCF_{it}) \times \log(Production_{it})] \\ &+ \beta_9 \log(ECI_{i,t-1}) \\ &+ \epsilon_{it} \end{aligned} $$
Note: Includes lagged dependent variable to capture persistence in economic complexity. Standard errors clustered by country.

Model 4: Fully Lagged Dynamic Panel

$$ \begin{aligned} \log(ECI_{it}) = \beta_0 &+ \beta_1 \log(HCI_{i,t-1}) + \beta_2 \log(GFCF_{i,t-1}) + \beta_3 \log(Production_{i,t-1}) \\ &+ \beta_4 PoliticalStability_{i,t-1} + \beta_5 RuleOfLaw_{i,t-1} + \beta_6 Trade_{i,t-1} \\ &+ \beta_7 [\log(HCI_{i,t-1}) \times \log(Production_{i,t-1})] \\ &+ \beta_8 [\log(GFCF_{i,t-1}) \times \log(Production_{i,t-1})] \\ &+ \beta_9 \log(ECI_{i,t-1}) \\ &+ \epsilon_{it} \end{aligned} $$
Note: All explanatory variables are lagged one period to address endogeneity and reverse causality concerns. Interaction terms created from mean-centered lagged variables. Standard errors clustered by country.

Variable Definitions

  • ECIit: Economic Complexity Index for country i at time t
  • HCIit: Human Capital Index
  • GFCFit: Gross Fixed Capital Formation (% of GDP)
  • Productionit: Total mineral production value per capita
  • PoliticalStabilityit: Political Stability and Absence of Violence index
  • RuleOfLawit: Rule of Law index
  • Tradeit: Trade openness (% of GDP)
  • εit: Error term

Estimation Details

  • Method: Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)
  • Standard Errors: Clustered by country to account for within-country correlation
  • Sample: Resource-rich countries (mineral production ≥ $10 per capita)
  • Centering: Interaction terms use mean-centered variables to reduce multicollinearity
