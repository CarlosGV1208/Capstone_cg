Section 7

Econometrics Approach

Theoretical Background:

  • Structural transformation & industrial upgrading: Economic Complexity and value-added upgrading (Hidalgo & Hausmann, 2009), combined with the Growth Diagnostics Framework (Hausmann, Rodrik & Velasco, 2005).
  • Institutions and development: Institutional economics framework to assess how governance quality shapes development outcomes (Acemoglu & Robinson, 2012).
  • Natural resource management: Natural Resource Wealth Management literature to identify conditions for successful management of resource rents (van der Ploeg & Venables, 2012).

Panel Data Fixed Effects Model

$$ ECI_{it} = \alpha + \beta_1 NR_{it} + \beta_2 Cap_{it} + \beta_3 (NR_{it} \times Cap_{it}) + \beta_4 Trade_{it} + \beta_5 Inst_{it} + FE_{i} + \epsilon_{it} $$

Variable Definitions:

ECI: Economic Complexity
NR: Natural Resource Rents
Cap: Capital accumulation
Trade: Trade Openness
Inst: Institutional Quality
FE: Country Fixed Effects
